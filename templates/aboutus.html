Our project “College Application” is a web application, which provides a simple interface for student-faculty information. It can be used by educational institutes or colleges to maintain the records of students easily. It handles all the academic and non-academic activities of an academic institute which can be accessed by the student and the faculty.


This is a college website which is designed to aid students to properly plan their absentism, study schedules, facilitates to explore all the events taking place in the college, collaborate with like-minded students to study in a group and receive the mentoring they need. The details are protected, user-friendly and enabled convenient access to different kinds of information and services
